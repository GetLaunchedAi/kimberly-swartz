About Me

Seeking Beauty & Connection


Multi-disciplinary Artist & Traveler


My interest is in beauty and connection. I see it everywhere and I look for it in everyone. I document it in photography and paint. I write about it, I create it when I see an opening. I see beauty in the lines on someone's face and the depth of their stories. Not all stories are beautiful, but the strength to tell them is amazing. I am a life-long multi-disciplinary artist, working in whatever medium is turning me on at the moment.
