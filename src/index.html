About Us

A Welcoming Pentecostal Church


Located in Livonia, MI


Open Arms Church is a Pentecostal church affiliated with the Assemblies of God, pastored by Reverend Gradyn B. Jensen. We are committed to creating a warm, inclusive family atmosphere where people of all backgrounds can experience God's love, grow in their faith, and build meaningful relationships with one another.

  • Warm, Inclusive Family Atmosphere
  • Interactive Children's Ministry Programs
  • Community Outreach - Food Pantries & School Supply Drives
  • Welcoming Environment for All
  • Family-Friendly Congregation
