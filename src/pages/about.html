About Open Arms Church

Open Arms Church is a Pentecostal church in Livonia, Michigan, affiliated with the Assemblies of God. We are committed to creating a warm, inclusive family atmosphere where people of all backgrounds can experience God's love, grow in their faith, and build meaningful relationships with one another.

Under the leadership of Pastor Reverend Gradyn B. Jensen, we strive to provide inspiring worship, meaningful teaching from God's Word, and opportunities for fellowship and service. Our church family welcomes visitors and new members with open arms, creating an environment where everyone can find community and grow in their relationship with God.
