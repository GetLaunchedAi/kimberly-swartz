About Me

About Kimberly Swartz

My interest is in beauty and connection. I see it everywhere and I look for it in everyone. I document it in photography and paint. I write about it, I create it when I see an opening. I see beauty in the lines on someone's face and the depth of their stories. Not all stories are beautiful, but the strength to tell them is amazing.

I am a life-long multi-disciplinary artist. I work in whatever medium is turning me on at the moment. I am a tribal elder with the Turtle Lake St. Croix Chippewa tribe in Wisconsin. Not having been raised in the culture, I am excited to be learning about it. I am traveling full time now and am sharing my travels here. Come along for the ride! Who knows what we will encounter next?

Exploring beauty and connection through art, photography, and storytelling. Documenting moments, capturing stories, and creating when I see an opening.

Kimberly Swartz Multi-disciplinary Artist • Photographer • Traveler
