FAQ's

Art FAQs

Answers to common questions about my art, artistic process, and how I work as a multi-disciplinary artist.

  • As a multi-disciplinary artist, I work in whatever medium is turning me on at the moment. This might include painting, mixed media, or other artistic expressions. I explore different mediums based on what inspires me and what best serves the story or connection I'm trying to create.

  • My interest is in beauty and connection. I see it everywhere and I look for it in everyone. I create art that seeks to reveal something beautiful, capture a moment, or tell a story. Each piece aims to connect with the viewer on some level.

  • I create when I see an opening. I might be inspired by the lines on someone's face, the depth of their stories, or simply something beautiful I encounter. I work in whatever medium is turning me on at the moment, allowing inspiration to guide my artistic expression.

  • I'd be happy to discuss commission opportunities. Please contact me to discuss your vision and how we might work together to create something meaningful.

  • You can view my artwork in the gallery section of this website. I also share my work and process through my blog, the Repair Journal, where I document my travels and artistic journey.

I'd love to answer any questions you have about my artwork or artistic process. Contact me to learn more.
