Interactive Children's Ministry


Nurturing Young Hearts in Faith


At Open Arms Church, we believe that children are an important part of our church family. Our interactive children's ministry provides a warm, welcoming environment where kids can learn about God's love through age-appropriate teachings, fun activities, and meaningful connections.



What We Offer

  1. Age-Appropriate Lessons: Bible stories and teachings tailored to different age groups
  2. Interactive Activities: Games, crafts, and creative projects that reinforce biblical truths
  3. Safe Environment: Qualified volunteers who are passionate about nurturing children's faith

FAQ's

Children's Ministry FAQs

Answers to common questions about our children's ministry, programs, and what to expect when visiting Open Arms Church with your family.

  • Our children's ministry welcomes children of all ages with age-appropriate activities and lessons. We provide interactive programs designed to engage children and help them grow in their understanding of God's love.

  • Children's ministry is available during our Sunday morning service (9:00 AM - 12:30 PM) and our Wednesday evening service (7:00 PM - 8:30 PM). We welcome families to participate in our worship services together.

  • Yes, safety is our top priority. Our children's ministry is supervised by qualified, dedicated volunteers who are passionate about creating a safe and nurturing environment where children can learn and grow.

  • While advance registration is not required, we welcome you to contact us ahead of time if you have questions or would like more information about our children's ministry programs.

  • Our interactive approach includes hands-on activities, creative projects, games, and engaging lessons that help children actively participate in learning about faith, making the experience both fun and meaningful.
